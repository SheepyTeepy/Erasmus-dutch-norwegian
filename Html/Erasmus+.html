    • Erasmus +

    Erasmus+ is the European Union's program to support education, training, youth, and sport in Europe. It provides opportunities for individuals to study, train, gain experience, and volunteer abroad. The program aims to enhance skills and employability, as well as modernize education, training, and youth work across Europe.

    Erasmus+ offers a wide range of activities, including: Study abroad, Do intership in another country, Join exchange projects, Work together with school in other countries and Improve language and intercultural skills

    Main Goals of Erasmus+

    The main goals of Erasmus+ are to promote learning mobility, foster cooperation between educational institutions, and support policy development in the fields of education, training, youth, and sport. The program aims to enhance the quality and relevance of education and training systems, promote social inclusion and diversity, and encourage innovation and creativity.
